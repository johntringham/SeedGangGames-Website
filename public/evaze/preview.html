Evaze Preview Pack

Evaze is an upcoming puzzle-arcade mobile game for Android and iOS, designed and developed by Seed Gang Games.

Evaze combines the puzzle aspect of a traditional block-sliding game with the arcade action of Pacman, set inside an abstract minimalist space setting.

In Evaze, you are in a morphing 2 dimensional labyrinth on a grid, which you have to navigate through to pick up tokens and avoid enemies. The player swipes to push rows of tiles that make up the maze, which allows you to move the player, the enemies, the tokens and powerups, as well as creating (and destroying) pathways in the level. This leads to tactics like blocking off enemies so they can't reach you, or creating a routes to get to places you couldn't reach before.

A prototype for the game was made for Ludum Dare 2020, where it scored a 4.0/5 overall score (top 5% of the 3200 submissions), and was featured in Randomise User's list of Best Ludum Dare 47 Games. Since then, the game has been revamped into a fully fledged mobile game, ready for release for iOS and Android.
